Who We Are

Beachside Studios is a premium private recording facility located in the heart of Inglewood, CA. We blend state-of-the-art recording technology with a laid-back, highly creative environment.

Whether you're a solo artist capturing your next single, a full band, or a podcaster needing broadcast-quality sound, we provide the gear, the engineering expertise, and the atmosphere to bring your project to life. We operate 24 hours a day to match your creative schedule.
